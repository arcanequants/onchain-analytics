## 2. AI-Specific Risk Profile

### 2.1 Risk Categories

| Risk Category | Description | Severity | Likelihood |
|---------------|-------------|----------|------------|
| Algorithmic errors | AI provides incorrect analysis leading to financial loss | High | Medium |
| Data accuracy | Blockchain data errors cause incorrect insights | High | Low |
| Hallucinations | AI generates false information | Medium | Medium |
| Bias/discrimination | AI outputs show unintended bias | Medium | Low |
| IP infringement | AI outputs inadvertently infringe IP | Low | Low |
| Privacy violations | Improper handling of user data | High | Low |

### 2.2 Mitigation Measures

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Algorithmic errors | Disclaimers, confidence scores, human review prompts |
| Data accuracy | Multi-source verification, real-time validation |
| Hallucinations | Model guardrails, source linking, accuracy checks |
| Bias | Regular audits, demographic parity testing |
| IP infringement | Content filtering, output monitoring |
| Privacy | SOC 2 compliance, no PII collection |

## 3. Insurance Coverage Types

### 3.1 Professional Liability / E&O

**What it covers:**
- Claims from clients alleging negligent advice
- Errors in AI-generated analysis
- Failure to deliver promised services
- Breach of professional duty

**Relevance to AI Perception:**
- Users may rely on AI insights for trading decisions
- Incorrect analysis could lead to financial losses
- Claims of negligent advice or recommendations

**Recommended coverage:** $2M-$5M

### 3.2 Technology E&O

**What it covers:**
- Failures in technology products
- Software bugs and errors
- System downtime losses
- Data integrity issues

**Relevance to AI Perception:**
- Platform downtime during critical market events
- Bugs in analytics algorithms
- API failures affecting client systems

**Recommended coverage:** $2M-$5M

### 3.3 Cyber Liability

**What it covers:**
- Data breaches
- Ransomware attacks
- Privacy violations
- Notification costs
- Forensic investigations
- Business interruption

**Relevance to AI Perception:**
- Customer data (emails, preferences)
- API credentials
- Platform security breaches

**Recommended coverage:** $2M-$5M

### 3.4 AI-Specific Insurance

**What it covers:**
- AI/ML algorithm failures
- Autonomous decision liabilities
- Training data issues
- Model drift consequences
- Explainability failures

**Relevance to AI Perception:**
- AI-generated insights leading to losses
- Model accuracy degradation
- Failure to explain AI decisions

**Recommended coverage:** $1M-$2M

### 3.5 Media Liability

**What it covers:**
- Copyright infringement
- Defamation
- Content-related claims

**Relevance to AI Perception:**
- AI might generate content infringing IP
- Newsletter/report content issues

**Recommended coverage:** $500K-$1M

## 6. Key Policy Terms

### 6.1 Terms to Negotiate

| Term | Preferred | Why Important |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| Retroactive date | Full prior acts | Covers historical development |
| Extended reporting | 3+ years | Time to discover claims |
| Worldwide coverage | Yes | Users from all jurisdictions |
| Regulatory coverage | Included | AI regulations evolving |
| Defense costs | Outside limits | Doesn't erode coverage |
| Hammer clause | 50/50 or better | Control over settlements |

### 6.2 Common Exclusions

| Exclusion | Risk Level | Mitigation |
|-----------|------------|------------|
| Known claims | Medium | Disclose all potential issues |
| Intentional acts | Low | Standard exclusion |
| Criminal acts | Low | Standard exclusion |
| War/terrorism | Low | Standard exclusion |
| Nuclear/radiation | N/A | Standard exclusion |
| Prior/pending litigation | Varies | Ensure clean disclosure |

### 6.3 AI-Specific Exclusions to Watch

| Exclusion | Risk | Negotiation |
|-----------|------|-------------|
| Algorithmic discrimination | Medium | Push for coverage with guardrails |
| Training data IP | Low | Document data sources |
| Autonomous decisions | High | Clarify human-in-loop |
| Model drift | Medium | Regular retraining clause |

## 7. Application Requirements

### 7.1 Common Questions

Insurers will ask about:

1. **Business Overview**
   - Revenue (current and projected)
   - Customer count
   - Services offered
   - Industries served

2. **Technology**
   - AI/ML models used
   - Data sources
   - Human oversight processes
   - Accuracy monitoring

3. **Security**
   - SOC 2 status (advantage: we have it)
   - Security controls
   - Incident history
   - Vendor management

4. **Risk Management**
   - Terms of service
   - Disclaimers
   - Customer contracts
   - Dispute history

## 8. Quote Request Process

### 8.1 Recommended Approach

1. **Prepare documentation** (1-2 days)
   - Gather all required documents
   - Complete risk profile summary

2. **Request quotes from 3 providers** (same day)
   - Coalition (tech-focused)
   - Embroker (startup package)
   - Founder Shield (crypto experience)

3. **Review proposals** (1 week)
   - Compare coverage terms
   - Identify gaps and exclusions
   - Negotiate improvements

4. **Select and bind** (1-2 days)
   - Finalize policy terms
   - Pay premium
   - Receive certificates

### 8.2 Quote Request Template

```
Subject: Insurance Quote Request - AI Analytics Platform

Dear [Provider],

We are seeking quotes for technology and AI-related insurance coverage for AI Perception, an AI-powered blockchain analytics platform.

Business Overview:
- AI-powered analytics platform for cryptocurrency data
- Launched: 2024
- Projected ARR: $500K-$1M
- Customer base: Individual traders, researchers, crypto projects
- SOC 2 Type II compliant

Coverage Sought:
- Technology E&O: $2M-$5M
- Cyber Liability: $2M-$5M
- General Liability: $1M-$2M
- AI-specific coverage (if available)

Key Information:
- AI Models: OpenAI GPT-4, Anthropic Claude (API-based, no training on customer data)
- Data: Public blockchain data only, no PII
- Disclaimers: Comprehensive terms of service with investment disclaimer
- Security: SOC 2 Type II, encryption, access controls

Please provide:
1. Quote with coverage options
2. Available deductible options
3. Key exclusions
4. Timeline to bind

Attached:
- Business summary
- Terms of Service
- SOC 2 summary
- Security overview

Best regards,
[Name]
CFO, AI Perception
```

## Appendix A: Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| E&O | Errors and Omissions (Professional Liability) |
| Occurrence policy | Covers incidents during policy period |
| Claims-made policy | Covers claims made during policy period |
| Retroactive date | Earliest date covered by claims-made policy |
| Hammer clause | Provision about consent to settle |
| Sublimit | Lower limit for specific coverage within policy |
| Aggregate limit | Maximum payout per policy period |
| Per-occurrence limit | Maximum payout per incident |
