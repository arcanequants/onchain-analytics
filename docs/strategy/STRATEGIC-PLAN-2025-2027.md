## 1. Executive Summary

AI Perception is positioning itself as the definitive platform for understanding and improving how brands are perceived by AI systems. As AI increasingly mediates consumer decisions, brand visibility in AI responses becomes critical to business success.

### Vision (2027)

**"Every brand knows and optimizes their AI perception."**

### Mission

To help brands understand, measure, and improve how they appear in AI-generated responses, ensuring they remain visible and accurately represented as AI becomes the primary interface between consumers and information.

### 3-Year Goals

|------|------------|-------|-----------------|
| 2025 | $500K | 2,000 | Category creator |
| 2026 | $2M | 10,000 | Category leader |
| 2027 | $8M | 50,000 | Dominant platform |

## 4. Strategic Bets

### Bet 1: AI-First Marketing Becomes Mainstream (Confidence: 85%)

**Thesis**: By 2026, "AI Optimization" will be as important as "SEO" for brand visibility.

**Investment**: 40% of R&D budget
**Pivot trigger**: <20% of prospects understand the value proposition by Q2 2025

### Bet 2: Enterprise Adoption Accelerates (Confidence: 70%)

**Thesis**: Large enterprises will require AI perception monitoring for governance and brand protection.

**Investment**: 25% of R&D budget
**Pivot trigger**: <10 enterprise leads by Q4 2025

### Bet 3: Multi-Modal AI Creates New Opportunities (Confidence: 60%)

**Thesis**: Image, video, and voice AI will require brand perception analysis, expanding TAM.

**Investment**: 15% of R&D budget
**Pivot trigger**: Multi-modal AI not gaining consumer traction by 2026

### Bet 4: Geographic Expansion Drives Growth (Confidence: 75%)

**Thesis**: Non-English markets will adopt AI search, creating localization opportunities.

**Investment**: 20% of R&D budget
**Pivot trigger**: <5% of revenue from non-US markets by Q4 2025
