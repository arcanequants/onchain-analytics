## ğŸ¨ Design Philosophy

### Core Principles Applied

1. **Contrast Hierarchy**
   - Text: 4.5:1 minimum (WCAG AA)
   - Interactive elements: 3:1 minimum
   - Decorative: No minimum (but still visible)

2. **Information Architecture**
   - Most important: Largest, boldest, highest contrast
   - Secondary: Medium size, medium weight
   - Tertiary: Smallest, lightest

3. **Semantic Colors**
   - Green: Positive change, success, growth
   - Red: Negative change, danger, warning
   - Blue/Gold: Accent, interactive, highlight
   - Gray: Neutral, informational

4. **Spatial Relationships**
   - Cards: Elevated with subtle shadows
   - Borders: 1-2px, soft colors
   - Spacing: Generous (20-24px gaps)
   - Padding: 20-32px for cards
